About This Platform

This learning system is designed to help students master web development through structured modules and exams.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accessible, practical, and high-quality web development education for students at all levels — from complete beginners to those looking to sharpen their professional skills.

📚 What You'll Learn

Our curriculum covers HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript through real-world examples, interactive exercises, and module quizzes — building up to a final certification exam.

🏆 Certification

Students who score 80% or higher on any final exam receive a downloadable certificate of completion, recognizing their achievement in that technology.

🌐 Bilingual Support

All courses and exams are fully available in both English and Spanish, making this platform ideal for bilingual classrooms and self-learners around the world.
